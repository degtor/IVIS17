ENVIROMIND

Brings the world together

About

Enviromind visualises the relationship between countries CO2 emissions and their trade balance in order to reveal international connections and relationships that affects both the global economy and the environment, providing historic and economical context to the climate debate. This makes it possible to see how historic events have made an impact on several different countries economy and emissions and helps the user to understand how globalised the world really is.
