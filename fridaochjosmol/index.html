ENVIROMIND

Brings the world together

About

Climate change is a global issue in a globalised world but responsibility is often distributed on a national level. Each country should meet the same climate goals regardless of their prerequisites.


Talking about emissions and climate change can often make you think of big vehicles in heavy traffic and steaming industrial chimneys. We often discuss how production and transportation affect climate change but fail to take the trade between countries into account.


Enviromind visualizes both countries' CO2 emissions and trade balances in order to reveal potential relationships between the environmental impact and the economy. By showing the top export and import countries for each country international relationships can also be seen and a global perspective can be obtained.
